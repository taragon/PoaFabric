Who are we?

Poafabric is a platform connecting recyclers and upcyclers. Recyclers can list items for reuse, while upcyclers can find raw materials to transform into something beautiful. Join the community today and help reduce waste creatively!

Why Poafabric

We bring together creatives and sustainability champions to make impact through upcycling. Be part of the change.
